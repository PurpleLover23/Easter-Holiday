What is Easter?

Easter is an event which includes traditions such as Easter Egg Hunt, Spring Garden Parties etc. The most fun part of this event is decorating with spring flowers, crafting and hosting eventful games for anyone to include themselves in to.

Easter is just not all about the fun times and traditions that go by, it is also about the Christians who celebrates this event to honour the resurrection of Jesus Christ from his death and therefore it is considered to be an important even in their faith.
